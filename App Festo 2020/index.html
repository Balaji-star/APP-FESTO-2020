About the event

The M-APPS Club of Sri Sairam Engineering College, Chennai in collaboration with MIT Square, London announces the Mobile/Web App Competition to recognize creativity and excellence among its students and motivate them to commercialize their research/learning outcomes to accelerate the economic and societal benefits.

AppFesto 2020

Theme

To innovate Mobile/web “Applications on Post Pandemic Effect and Global Recovery

Scope of Mobile/Web app

Mobile/web apps that are based on any concept, principle, technique, or application related to the theme will be accepted. Examples could be in the area of education, healthcare, e-commerce and community development (charitable activities). We encourage students to use the latest programming languages/technologies such as Kotlin, Flutter, Swift, React, Ionic, D3, flask and so on.

Competition Process

The Best Prepartion for Tomorrow is doing your Best Today

Protocol

Teams must submit a proposal (no more than 2 pages) describing their mobile/web app via email to the chairs of the competition steering committee by August 17, 2020.
Email: appfesto2020@gmail.com

The proposal must include

  • A list of team members and their affiliation

  • A problem statement of why the app is necessary to the community & the theme

  • A list of features of the mobile/web app

  • The target market segment(s) of the mobile/web app

  • Any other relevant information & block diagrams/flowcharts can be included

Teams must submit

  • A prototype of their mobile/web app

  • A software design document

  • A demonstration video, via email to the chairs of the competition steering committee by September 30, 2020

  • The competition steering committee will evaluate the submissions and select a maximum number of four teams as finalists.

The selected finalist teams will present the app virtually or in person (based on the current situation) to present their mobile/web app.

The presentation will include

  • Technical details of the mobile/web app

  • A business plan to justify the need for their mobile/web apps in the marketplace

  • A demonstration of the prototype
